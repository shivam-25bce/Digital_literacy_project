## Case Study: UPI Fraud

UPI fraud is a common cybercrime where users are tricked into sending money through fake payment requests. In many cases, fraudsters send a request claiming it is for receiving money, but when the user enters the PIN, money gets deducted.

Typically, scammers contact users via phone or social media and ask them to approve a payment request. Many students and new users fall victim due to lack of awareness.

The consequences include financial loss and data misuse. Victims often find it difficult to recover their money.
